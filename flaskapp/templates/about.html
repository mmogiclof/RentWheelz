About Rent Wheelz

Rent Wheelz is a car rental service that allows you to rent a car for a day, a week, or even a month. We have a wide range of cars to choose from, so you can find the perfect car for your needs. Our service is fast, easy, and convenient, so you can get on the road in no time. Rent Wheelz is the best way to get around town, whether you need a car for work, leisure, or just to get from A to B. Rent Wheelz - your ride, your way.


API Endpoints

  • Cars
    • GET /api/v1/cars - Get a list of all available cars
    • GET /api/v1/cars/{id} - Get a specific car
    • POST /api/v1/cars/create - Create a car
    • DELETE /api/v1/cars/{id} - Delete a car
  • Users
    • GET /api/v1/users - Get a list of all available users
    • GET /api/v1/users/{id} - Get a specific user
    • GET /api/v1/users/{id}/bookings - Get a list of bookings for the user
    • DELETE /api/v1/users/{id} - Delete a user
  • Reservations
    • GET /api/v1/reservations - Get a list of all available reservations
    • GET /api/v1/reservations/{id} - Get a specific reservation
    • POST /api/v1/reservations/create -Create a reservation
    • DELETE /api/v1/reservations/{id} - Delete a reservation
